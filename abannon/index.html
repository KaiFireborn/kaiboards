Abannon - The Tragedy's first Part

An indifferent, unmotivated adventurer follows the call of temptation for the first time in forever, and ends up unsealing an evil that ravaged Heaven's hosts ten years prior. With the former seal keeper's unknowing friendship and healing, she sets off as a harbinger of calamity...
Yet one question remains unanswered for her even as they journey: what is her place between the mortals and the Higher Beings, the mages and the other Realms, the Artists and the dead?
